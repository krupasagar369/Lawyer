About Me

Dasari Sai Sreenivasulu

I am a qualified advocate with M.B.A. and L.L.B. degrees, practicing law in Nellore, Andhra Pradesh. With extensive experience in various areas of law, I am committed to providing excellent legal services to my clients.

I offer legal assistance in civil, criminal, family, property, and business cases. My clients can consult me for legal advice, filing of cases, documentation, and representation in court. I believe in providing sound counsel, adjudicating disputes justly, and offering complete support to individuals who need legal assistance.

Through on-time response and individualized attention, I help clients navigate their legal concerns with confidence. My chambers are conveniently located at GPR Grand Kalyanamandapam, making it easily accessible for consultations.
